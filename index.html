Vinyl Days by Logic (2022):

  • Overall, very solid album and significantly better than his music pre-retirement
  • Good mix of chill vibes and hype songs, while also sticking to a consistent sound that pertains to the theme of the album
  • Funkmaster Flex did a good job with the ad libs throughout the album
  • The features were all very good, and very well utilized, although I might say every feature outperformed Logic himself on every song, but I guess that's a good problem to have
  • 30 tracks is way too long and the album feels like it dragged on, despite the songs being relatively short
  • I actually haven't finished listening but this last track is 10 minutes so... Yeah
  • Overall album rating: 7.3/10
